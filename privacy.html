Privacy & Disclaimer

FitFlow stores only minimal data in your browser's chrome.storage.sync:

  • Theme preference (warm/dark)
  • Favourites list (exercise IDs)
  • Your “My Flow” plan (exercise IDs and names)

No personal identifiers, analytics, or tracking are collected. YouTube links open a new tab to search results; no API calls are made by default.

Incognito

If you enable “Allow in incognito”, Chrome uses a separate storage area. Your plan and favourites do not carry over between normal and incognito modes unless you recreate them there.

Medical Disclaimer

“Pregnancy Suitability” labels mean: Safe, Needs Caution, or Not Safe. These are general guidelines only and may not apply to every individual. Always consult a qualified professional before starting or modifying an exercise program.
